Milwaukee Life

Milwaukee is a city in the U.S. state of Wisconsin on Lake Michigan's western shore. It has a bustling nightlife, award-winning restaurants, lakefront museums and a thriving music and arts scene.History and tradition are embraced, celebrated with German fare, craft beer, Friday fish fries and an array of cultural events. Milwaukee hosts dozens of festivals each year. Summerfest, the city's biggest, spans a week and a half and welcomes hundreds of bands.
