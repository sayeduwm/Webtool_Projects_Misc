Scout Lake Park

Scout Lake Park excels as a great place to fish, with a nice paved path around the Lake, which is good for strolls, walkers and runners. The DNR stocks this Lake with fish, so fishing off the pier will yield bluegill, perch and rainbow trout. There's also a resident snapping turtle you may see here. The pier can get crowded with anglers on weekends, so offtimes are great for visiting.

There's an old County Parks property here, with men and women's bathrooms, and a small party room. There's not much green here to have a picnic, so Scout Lake probably wouldn't be the best place for your Stone Soul Picnic. I like the fishing here, so I'm sure I'll be back for many repeat trips. Located at 5902 West Loomis Rd Greendale, WI 53129

Fox Brook Park

Beautiful park! Activities include: swimming, diving board, fishing, paddle boats, play ground and picnic tables - all within 5 minute walk of each other . $4 cash only for parking.This may be the best random yelp selection in a long while.

This park boasts lost of trails (paved and unpaved) a dog park, an art gallery, a large (very cool) children play area, a covered venue with pic nic tables and fountains. Located at 2925 N Barker Rd Brookfield, WI 53045
