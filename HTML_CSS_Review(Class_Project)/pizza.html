Papa John's Pizza

Papa John's Pizza is an American pizza restaurant franchise. It runs the fourth largest pizza delivery restaurant chain in the United States, with headquarters in Jeffersontown, Kentucky, a suburb of Louisville.

Papa John's primarily takes carryout and delivery orders, although some stores have tables and chairs for dining in.The pizza chain has tight ties with football around the country. They have the naming rights to the University of Louisville’s Cardinal Stadium. They have a multi-year sponsorship with the National Football League. They’re the official pizza of the Arizona Cardinals, Atlanta Falcons, Baltimore Ravens, Dallas Cowboys, Denver Broncos, Houston Texans, Indianapolis Colts, Miami Dolphins, New York Giants, New York Jets, Philadelphia Eagles, Seattle Seahawks, St. Louis Rams, Tennessee Titans and Washington Redskins.
